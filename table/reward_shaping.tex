\small
\caption{Reward components used for teacher policy training.}
\label{tab:reward_terms}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.15}
\resizebox{0.38\textwidth}{!}{%

\begin{tabularx}{\columnwidth}{c c X}
\hline
\textbf{Term} & \textbf{Name} & \textbf{Purpose} \\
\hline
$r_{\text{dist}}$ & Hand distance &
Encourages the hand to approach the object. \\

$r_{\text{curl}}$ & Finger curl &
Biases fingers toward a grasp-ready configuration. \\

$r_{\text{align}}$ & Palm alignment &
Aligns palm orientation with the palm-down direction. \\

$r_{\text{contact}}$ & Contact bonus &
Rewards stable contact involving thumb and fingers. \\

$r_{\text{near}}$ & Stay-close &
Encourages the hand to remain close after reaching the object. \\

$r_{\text{goal}}$ & Goal tracking &
Encourages moving the grasped object toward the goal pose. \\

$r_{\text{lift}}$ & Lift &
Rewards vertical lifting after successful contact. \\

$r_{\text{action}}$ & Action smoothness &
Penalizes rapid action changes for stable control. \\
\hline
\end{tabularx}
}
